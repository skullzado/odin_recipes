Pork Chao Fan

Description

Pork Chao Fan is a Filipino style of the classic Chinese fried rice dish.

Put it in a bowl, and eat. Simple and belly-filling.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces ground pork or grilled pork belly
  • 4 cups rice (leftover)
  • 1/2 cup carrot (chopped)
  • 3/4 cup grean peas
  • 1 piece onion (minced)
  • 2 pieces eggs (beaten)
  • 1/2 cup green onions (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Sesame oil
  • Salt and ground white pepper to taste
  • 5 tablespoons cooking oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok. Add beaten eggs. Fry until eggs are fully cooked. Remove eggs from the pan. Set aside.
  2. Saut√© onion using remaining oil until it softens.
  3. Add ground pork. Cook while stirring until pork turns medium brown.
  4. Pour soy sauce, Sesame oil, and hoisin sauce into the wok. Stir.
  5. Add carrots and leftover rice. Stir-fry for 2 minutes. Toss until all ingredients are well-distributed.
  6. Add green peas and green onions. Cook for 1 minutes.
  7. Add the chopped fried egg and then season with salt and ground white pepper.
  8. Serve warm. Share and enjoy!
